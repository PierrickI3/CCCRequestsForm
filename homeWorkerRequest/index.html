Welcome to the Rapid Response Request Form for COVID-19 offers

This form should be used for the validation of solution designs for all COVID-19 offers. It is not designed to be a replication of what existing customers have today.

Fill out the form as required. Some fields are automatically completed using your Genesys Cloud details. Press Submit once all the required fields have been completed.

Our COVID-19 offer is available to existing customers and prospects in good standing (Direct or Indirect). Direct existing customers will get free PS services as per our defined package. Indirect existing customers via partners will get benefit of COVID-19 commercial offer, but they will have to deliver PS services on their own (exceptions require approval).

WebRTC should be used unless there are special circumstances preventing this. User bandwidth is expected to be in high demand due to additional family members consuming services, e.g. Video on Demand, Gaming, etc. Customers should perform "spot-tests" on as many home workers as possible using the Genesys Network Assessment Tool. IVRs will be limited to simple call handling trees for routing and skill identification will be included. CRM integration, as well as other back integrations, will not be part of this deployment

Number porting is not possible with this offer. Corporate VPN’s may require additional technical investigation and configuration.

Genesys supports:

  • Forwarding of existing numbers to Genesys Cloud Voice
  • Provisioning of new numbers with Genesys Cloud Voice
  • SIP trunk connections to existing infrastructure (BYOC Cloud)
  • SIP trunks direct to carriers (BYOC Cloud)

General Configuration


PS Engagement

  1. Collect the 4 main roles at the customer side: Our Care organization is working to automate the enrollment of our customer into the care and training process and to do so, it is mandatory to have the right contacts identified in SFDC.
  2. Introduce the PS Questionnaire to your customer - this makes sure that the customer starts collecting the information required by our PS to deploy the platform without any additional delay. Please inform the customer that this information is essential to complete the deployment of Genesys Cloud.
    1. Click here to download the latest PS Questionnaire.
    2. Click here to download a user guide on how to use the PS Questionnaire.
      3. To download the files below from the Genesys Cloud app, press SHIFT while clicking on each link.
  3. Make sure that the customer acts on the activation e-mail he receives once the order is processed – any delay in this step may seriously jeopardize the 48 hour deployment time.
    1. Raise awareness to the customer that he will receive this e-mail and that before he activates the org, PS cannot start the deployment.
    2. Make him aware that he will be asked to provide a name for his org in this process – this name cannot be changed afterwards, so he might not want to refer to Covid 19 in the name – just in case he wants to keep the org.
  4. Assist the customer with his network readiness assessment. PS is currently preparing a short video tutorial on the network readiness assessment, which we will share with you, once it's available.

Please engage PS in each of these deals as for now they will do the deployment – also for indirect customers. Our partner & alliance management teams are currently identifying selected partners who will be enabled to deploy this offer, but as long as these partners are not identified and announced, PS will do the deployment to be sure we deploy within the promised time and within the limit of the scope for this offer. SWAT teams have been created by PS in each region and engagement rules for PS have been defined and communicated on a regional basis. Please contact your local CSD if in doubt.
